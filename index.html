About Smoking Chicken Fish

Blending satire with blockchain innovation, Smoking Chicken Fish (SCF) is a legally registered church that worships Lord Fishnu and follows the 10 Chickenmandments. Beyond the absurdity, SCF empowers token holders to govern its direction and improve the lives of its community. At Barton Base Games, we bring this playful spirit to life with retro-inspired arcade games that entertain and engage our community.
